Our Story

We are Ecopolar, an eco-friendly company selling quality products, but also raising money for polar bears, since the ice caps are melting, which means they don’t have anywhere else to live. We would be ever-so grateful if you buy our products, and doing so would change how animals and polar bears alike, will live, impacting their lives, and letting them live in peace.

Starting as a side project selling a few sustainable items, we've grown into a small but dedicated brand that designs playful, high-quality eco-products. Every item is chosen or created to be plastic-free (or plastic-minimal), long-lasting, and produced responsibly — while directly funding polar bear protection.

Fun fact: Our name combines "eco" (for the environment) and "polar" (for the bears and Arctic we aim to help keep cool). Our motto — “Cooler choices for a cooler Arctic” — reminds us (and our customers) that small, smarter decisions can help slow warming and protect icy habitats.

Our Charity Partner: Born Free

We proudly partner with Born Free, an international wildlife charity founded in 1984 that's famous for its work keeping wildlife in the wild. Born Free campaigns against keeping wild animals in captivity where they suffer, rescues animals in need, and protects endangered species in their natural habitats.

For polar bears specifically, Born Free has led major efforts including:

  • The groundbreaking 2023 report Born to Roam: The Suffering of Polar Bears in Zoos, which exposed welfare issues for the ~300 polar bears held in captivity worldwide and called for an end to keeping them in zoos due to stress, unnatural enclosures, and public safety risks.
  • Advocacy for phasing out polar bear captivity entirely, as no zoo can meet their vast roaming and hunting needs.
  • Work to protect wild polar bears from climate change, habitat loss, and human-wildlife conflict.

Every purchase on Ecopolar contributes directly to Born Free's polar bear programs — helping fund habitat conservation, anti-captivity campaigns, and real-world protection in the Arctic. We're proud to support an organization that aligns perfectly with our belief: polar bears belong in the wild, roaming free on sea ice, not in concrete enclosures.

Our Core Values & Commitment

  • → Responsible Consumption: We support UN SDG 12 by promoting products that last, reduce waste, and avoid single-use plastics.
  • → Transparency: Clear pricing, honest sourcing, and open reporting on how much we donate (currently 20–30% of profits go to Born Free, depending on product).
  • → Fun & Accessibility: Conservation shouldn't feel boring — our cute, affordable items make eco-living joyful and shareable.
  • → Climate Awareness: We educate buyers about how everyday choices (like ditching plastic toothbrushes) help slow Arctic warming and protect sea ice for polar bears.
